# Client briefing template (LLM uses this for the meeting prep doc)

Generate a SHORT briefing (≤ 250 words) covering:

1. **Company snapshot** (1-2 sentences)
   - Industry, approximate size, geography if known
   - Public information only — no guesses or fabricated details
   - If the job is posted anonymously, say so explicitly

2. **Likely pain point** (1-2 sentences)
   - Inferred from the job description, in their context
   - Distinguish what they *said* from what you *infer*

3. **Decision maker hypothesis** (1 sentence)
   - Who is likely behind this post: ESG Manager? Head of Asset Mgmt? IT/BI lead?
   - Bias toward technical readers if the post uses technical language;
     bias toward business readers if the post focuses on outcomes.

4. **Key questions to ask in discovery call** (3 bullets max)
   - Tailored to their post — not the generic 6 from the framework.
   - Each question should yield specific budget/scope information.

5. **Red flags** (1 sentence — or "None obvious")
   - Things that suggest the job might be a bad fit, scope-creep risk,
     pay risk, or unrealistic expectations.

## Tone
Internal briefing tone — like a senior partner briefing a junior on a client.
Direct, no fluff, no apologies, no hedging beyond what is genuinely uncertain.

## Format
Plain text with the 5 numbered sections. No bold/italic. No emojis.
