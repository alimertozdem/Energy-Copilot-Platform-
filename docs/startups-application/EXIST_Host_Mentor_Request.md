2. **Mentor role.** May I name you as the academic mentor on the application? This involves a brief mentor statement confirming your academic/technical support; I'll draft it for your review so it takes minimal time on your side.

To make this easy I've prepared a **one-page summary** of the venture and the 12-month plan, plus a fuller application package — happy to send them ahead or walk you through them whenever suits you.
