# EnergyLens — Go-To-Market Strategy

**Bottom-up SaaS motion for EU commercial real estate energy intelligence.**

---

## 1. GTM Philosophy

EnergyLens follows a **bottom-up, validation-first** GTM model:

1. **Start with the cheapest, most accessible customers** (universities, mid-market) to validate
2. **Use case studies to enter higher-value segments** (property mgmt, hotels, REITs)
3. **Avoid enterprise sales until product is mature** (24+ months in)
4. **Bootstrap-friendly economics** — CAC < €500, LTV > €5,000, payback < 6 months

**Why bottom-up?** Solo founder can't afford 6-month enterprise sales cycles. Mid-market and academic pilots close in 2-8 weeks.

## 2. Target Customer Segmentation

### Tier 1 priority (first 6 months)

#### Segment A: University Campuses
- **Profile:** Public/private universities with sustainability goals
- **Pain:** Manual reporting for accreditation (DGNB, BREEAM), no real-time visibility
- **Pitch:** "Academic case study + free pilot in exchange for testimonial"
- **Entry:** Founder's network (BSBI, Yaşar Univ alumni)
- **Examples:** BSBI Berlin, HU Berlin, TU Berlin, Frankfurt UAS
- **Deal size:** €0-€3k/year (free → academic pricing)
- **Sales cycle:** 2-6 weeks
- **First target:** BSBI as M.Sc. capstone (Q3 2026)

#### Segment B: Mid-Market Property Management
- **Profile:** Firms managing 5-50 buildings (mixed offices, retail, residential)
- **Pain:** Excel-based reporting, CRREM compliance pressure, ESG reporting load
- **Pitch:** "€299/building/month replaces your spreadsheets + ensures EU compliance"
- **Entry:** LinkedIn outreach, IFM/CAFM forums, BSBI alumni network
- **Examples:** Property management arms of local REITs, family-office portfolios
- **Deal size:** €5k-€50k/year (depending on portfolio size)
- **Sales cycle:** 1-3 months
- **First target:** 1 Berlin property manager (Q4 2026)

### Tier 2 priority (months 6-18)

#### Segment C: Hotel Chains
- **Profile:** Mid-size DACH hotel chains (10-50 properties)
- **Pain:** 24/7 operations, high energy intensity, ESG reporting requirements
- **Pitch:** "Daily ops optimization + CSRD-ready ESG reporting"
- **Entry:** Hospitality industry events, IHA Germany membership
- **Deal size:** €20k-€100k/year per chain
- **Sales cycle:** 3-6 months

#### Segment D: Healthcare Facilities
- **Profile:** Hospital groups, clinic networks
- **Pain:** Critical loads, energy security, regulatory complexity
- **Pitch:** "Resilience + cost + compliance triangle"
- **Entry:** Yaşar University healthcare network, BSBI partner hospitals
- **Deal size:** €30k-€200k/year per network
- **Sales cycle:** 6-12 months

### Tier 3 priority (months 18+)

#### Segment E: REITs and Asset Managers
- **Profile:** Listed REITs, institutional CRE asset managers
- **Pain:** Portfolio-wide CRREM scoring, GRESB reporting, decarbonization planning
- **Pitch:** "Portfolio-wide compliance + carbon strategy"
- **Entry:** Microsoft Partner Network introductions, GRESB events
- **Deal size:** €100k-€1M+/year
- **Sales cycle:** 9-18 months (procurement-heavy)
- **Examples:** Aroundtown, Patrizia, Vonovia (residential adjacent)

#### Segment F: Shopping Malls (AVM)
- **Profile:** Mall operators (Sonae Sierra, ECE, Unibail-Rodamco)
- **Pain:** Common area energy, tenant comparison, sustainability marketing
- **Pitch:** "Tenant transparency + common area optimization"
- **Deal size:** €20k-€80k/year per mall
- **Sales cycle:** 6-12 months

## 4. Sales Process

### Stage 1: Discovery (Week 1)
**Channel:** LinkedIn message → 15-min discovery call
- "How many buildings do you manage?"
- "What's your current energy data process?"
- "Are you facing CRREM/CSRD/EnEfG pressure?"
- "What would a 10% energy savings mean for your portfolio?"

**Conversion gate:** Confirmed pain + decision authority

### Stage 2: Demo (Week 2)
**Channel:** 45-min Power BI live demo (Loom-recorded or live Teams)
- 5 min: Founder + EnergyLens intro
- 15 min: Live dashboard walkthrough (using their building type)
- 15 min: Specific value calculation (savings, compliance)
- 10 min: Q&A + next steps

**Conversion gate:** Stakeholder excited, willing to pilot

### Stage 3: Pilot Proposal (Week 3)
**Channel:** 1-page proposal + technical onboarding plan
- Pilot scope: 1-3 buildings, 3-month free trial
- Data requirements (CSV or API access)
- Success metrics (savings identified, time saved, compliance status)
- Conversion pricing (post-pilot)

**Conversion gate:** Signed pilot agreement (light contract)

### Stage 4: Pilot Onboarding (Week 4-6)
- Data ingestion setup
- Customer training (1 session)
- Customer Slack/Teams channel for support
- Weekly check-ins

### Stage 5: Pilot Review + Conversion (Month 3-4)
- Quantified results presentation
- Conversion proposal at standard or discounted pricing
- Multi-year discount if commits early

**Target conversion:** 50% of pilots → paying customers in first year.

## 5. Pricing Strategy

### Subscription tiers

| Tier | Price | Best for | Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Insight** | €99/building/month | SME offices, single buildings | Monthly KPIs, basic compliance, email reports |
| **Monitor** | €299/building/month | Mid-market property mgmt | All Insight + real-time alerts, weekly insights, IoT (Phase 2) |
| **Copilot** | €699/building/month | Enterprise, healthcare, hotels | All Monitor + AI recommendations, ML forecasting, expert routing |
| **Enterprise Custom** | €5k-50k/month | REITs, large portfolios | All Copilot + white-label, custom dashboards, dedicated support |

### Discount strategy

| Customer type | Discount | Logic |
|---|---|---|
| Pilot (first 3 months) | 100% free | Acquire reference cases |
| University / non-profit | 50% off | Brand building + case studies |
| Annual prepay | 15% off | Cash flow + retention |
| 10+ buildings | 20% off | Portfolio incentive |
| 50+ buildings | Custom (~30% off) | Enterprise economics |
| Strategic logo (REIT, named brand) | Up to 50% off | Sales accelerator |
