## C. Channel & language strategy (rationale)

- **English-first** because founder German is **B1** — fine for written (reviewed) reports, not yet
  for confident B2B German calls. Don't pose as a German-native service (it risks the trust the
  sample is built to earn).
- **Tension to hold:** the small-mid DE Hausverwaltung wedge is the *least* English-comfortable
  segment. English-first naturally leans toward larger / international / ESG-finance buyers (Malt,
  English-comfortable DE firms). The deep Hausverwaltung wedge unlocks with **(a)** improving German
  and/or **(b)** a German-native commercial partner — which also brings local network.
- **German written reports** are offered (low risk; drafted + founder-reviewed); German *calls* are
  not committed at B1.
