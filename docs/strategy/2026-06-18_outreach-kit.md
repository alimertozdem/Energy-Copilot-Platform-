## TRACK 2 — LinkedIn free pilot (validation → startup level)

### Who to target (decision-maker or close to it)
- DE **Hausverwaltung** / housing companies / commercial property owners & asset managers.
- **ESG / sustainability managers** at mid-size real-estate firms or funds.
- **Facility managers** of a single sizable building (office, hospital, hotel, campus).
- Filter: someone who can (a) grant data access and (b) give a reference. Skip pure influencers.

### The free-pilot message (EN)
> Hi [Name], I've built an analytics platform (EnergyLens) that turns a building's energy bills into
> an EU-compliance and decarbonisation picture — EPBD/GEG readiness, CRREM stranding, carbon cost, and
> subsidy-backed retrofit ROI. No hardware, no site visit.
>
> I'm looking for **one real building to run a full screening on, free**, to validate it at scale.
> In return I'd ask for your data, permission to use the (anonymised) results as a reference, and — if
> you find it useful — a short testimonial or intro. One building, ~2 weeks, fixed scope.
>
> Here's a sample of the output [PDF]. Open to it? — Ali Mert

### The exchange (don't skip — this is what makes "free" worth it)
- **You give:** one building's full EPBD/CO₂ readiness report (the Offer-2 deliverable), free.
- **They give:** 12 mo bills + building info · permission to use anonymised results as a
  reference/case study · a short testimonial + (ideally) 1–2 peer intros if satisfied.
- **Scope/time-box:** ONE building, standard deliverable, ~2 weeks. Anything more = a paid quote.

### Cadence (quality > volume — LinkedIn flags bulk)
- 5–10 **personalised** messages/week. Track in a simple sheet (name, company, sent, replied, follow-up).
- One follow-up after ~5 days. Stop at 2 touches if no reply.
