# EnergyLens — Conference Talking Points
metiundo "Energiewende konkret", Berlin, 2026-07-01 (Wednesday)

Cebinde dursun. Konuşulacak satırlar İngilizce; altındaki TR notlar ipucu/hatırlatma.

---

## 1. Opener (one line)
> "EnergyLens turns the energy data a building already has into a clear compliance status and a ranked, subsidized renovation plan. So an owner knows which building to fix first and how to pay for it, without new hardware or expensive consultants."

TR: Önce bu tek cümle. Sonra detaya in. Başlık = donanımsız uyumluluk + sübvansiyonlu retrofit kararı.

## 2. Who it is for (the wedge)
> "It is for small and mid-size building owners, and the property managers who run their buildings. They face new energy rules but have no energy team and cannot afford big consultants."

TR: Satış birimi = portföy. Küçük-orta Hausverwaltung. Ihmal edilen alt segment. IoT/kontrolü başa koyma.

## 3. Talking points (soru gelirse şunu söyle)

**What is it / whose problem?**
> "It answers the one question owners are stuck on: which building to fix first, what it costs, which subsidy pays for it, and how to prove they are compliant."

**Does it bring sustainability to small players?**
> "Yes, that is the point. Today this analysis is only affordable for big portfolios. We bring the same decisions to small and mid-size owners at a price they can pay."

**Is it scalable?**
> "The software scales, running one more building is almost free. The piece I am still solving is self-serve data onboarding, that is the step between a pilot and real scale."

**Would energy experts use it?**
> "Experts use it more as a tool to serve their own clients faster, or to refer clients in. The main user I build for is the non-expert owner or manager."
TR: Uzman = partner/kanal, ana alıcı değil.

**Can a non-expert use it?**
> "Yes, it is built for that. No jargon. You give your bills, it gives plain answers, and the copilot explains in normal language."

**Does it save staff or consultant cost?**
> "It replaces the expensive desk work, the analysis and reporting. Instead of hiring an in-house analyst or paying a consultant for every building, you get it for a subscription. It does not replace the people who do the physical renovation."
TR: "Elemanı kov" deme. "Pahalı masa-başı analizi otomatikleştiriyorum" de.

## 4. How it works, even from just bills
> "You do not need sensors or new hardware. You send the bills you already have, electricity and heating. From the bill we read the consumption, the cost, the period and the fuel. We add weather, grid carbon and prices ourselves. A short form covers the basics: area, type, age, and which systems the building has."
> "If you have almost no data, we still give a first result from your building type and size, marked as an estimate, and it sharpens as you add data. And we can show the projected result if you do the renovations we recommend."

TR: Katman bilerek böyle. (1) elinde ne varsa onunla, hiç yoksa tahminle; (2) simülasyon = yatırım yaparsan şu sonuç. Dürüst kal: tahmin aralıktır, projeksiyon modeldir, gerçek binada pilotla doğrulanıyor.

## 5. If they compare you (one-liners)
> Green Fusion / aedifion: "They control and optimize the heating system in real time, which needs hardware. We are the decision and compliance layer above it, no hardware, and we also work on buildings that will never have a BMS."
> Predium / Deepki: "Great for big portfolios with big budgets. We serve the smaller owners they do not reach, cheaper and self-serve."

TR: Kontrole girme. Donanımsızlığı güç olarak konumla, zayıflık değil.

## 6. Objection handlers
> "Do you control the building?" -> "No, on purpose. We tell you what to do and prove compliance. Control needs hardware and is a different job."
> "Can you prove it works?" -> "I am running first pilots now. Happy to show a live demo and a sample report." (demo: energy-copilot-platform.vercel.app/demo)
> "How accurate is it?" -> "Real data gives real results. With little data we show ranges, clearly marked, that tighten as data comes in."

TR: Pilot yok diye savunmaya geçme. "Bunun için kurdum, kanıtlıyorum" tonu. Biri bastırırsa demoyu aç.

## 7. Smart questions to ask them (networking + ihtiyaç ölçme)
> "How big is your portfolio, and are the EPBD or MEPS deadlines on your radar yet?"
> "Who handles your energy reporting today, in-house or a consultant?"
> "What is harder for you, knowing what to fix, or paying for it?"

TR: Sorarak hem doğal sohbet kurarsın hem gerçek acıyı öğrenirsin. Dinle, satma.

## 8. Words to know (term | what it is | TR ipucu)

|---|---|---|
| EPBD | EU Energy Performance of Buildings Directive | Renovasyon zorunluluklarının kaynağı. "ee-pee-bee-dee" |
| MEPS | Minimum Energy Performance Standards | En kötü binalara zorunlu asgari seviye (EPBD ile) |
| GEG | Gebäudeenergiegesetz | Almanya bina enerji yasası (ısıtma/yalıtım). "geh-eh-geh" |
| EPC / Energieausweis | Energy Performance Certificate | Binanın enerji karnesi (A-G) |
| EUI | Energy Use Intensity, kWh/m² per year | Ana benchmark metriği, enerji yoğunluğu |
| CRREM | Carbon Risk Real Estate Monitor | Karbon "stranding" yolu. "krem" okunur |
| Stranded asset | Kurala uymadığı için değer kaybeden bina | Sahibin korktuğu şey |
| ESG | Environmental, Social, Governance | Sürdürülebilirlik çerçevesi |
| CSRD | Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive | AB kurumsal raporlama yasası |
| ESRS E1 | CSRD iklim standardı | "ESRS-aligned" de, "CSRD-compliant" DEME (hukuki) |
| VSME | Voluntary SME sustainability standard | Küçükler için gönüllü standart |
| Scope 1/2/3 | Emisyon kapsamları | 1 doğrudan (gaz), 2 elektrik, 3 zincir/dolaylı |
| Primär-/Endenergie | Primary vs final energy | Final = sayaçtan geçen; primary = kayıplar dahil; GEG primary kullanır |
| HDD / CDD | Heating/Cooling Degree Days | Havayı tüketime göre düzeltir, derece-gün |
| BAFA | Federal subsidy office | Isı pompası, danışmanlık destekleri |
| KfW | State development bank | Retrofit kredisi/hibe |

| Sanierung / Retrofit | Building renovation/upgrade | Dämmung = yalıtım |
| Wärmepumpe | Heat pump | COP/SCOP = verim katsayısı |
| Hausverwaltung | Property management company | Senin kanalın. WEG = kat malikleri; Eigentümer = sahip |
| HKVO / CO2KostAufG | Heat-cost + CO2-cost allocation rules | Techem/ista; CO2 maliyetini kiracı-sahip paylaştırma |
| BMS / BAS / GLT | Building automation system | GLT = Almancası. Sende yok, bilerek |
| kWh / kW / kWp | Energy / power / solar peak | Tüketim / anlık güç / solar tepe |

| Energiewende | Germany's energy transition | Konferansın teması |
| Energieberater | Energy consultant (signs iSFP) | Senin kanal/partner adayın |
