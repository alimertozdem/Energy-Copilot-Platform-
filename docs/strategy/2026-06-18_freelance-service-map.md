# EnergyLens — Energy Freelance Service Map

> **Status:** DRAFT v1 — companion to [`2026-06-18_founder-operating-plan.md`](./2026-06-18_founder-operating-plan.md)
> **Date:** 2026-06-18
> **Purpose:** decide *which* energy-freelance services to productize through EnergyLens — and which
> to refuse. Grounded in 2026 market research (sources at end).

---

## 0. The decision this map forces

The ask was *"find the most-sold energy freelance services and build a system that does them all."*
**Rejected — that is the feature-trap in a new costume.** Two reframes:

1. The energy-freelance market is several **disjoint clusters** (analytics, reporting, engineering
   design, certified consulting, trading…). No single system does them all; trying = infinite build.
2. **You already built the system.** EnergyLens covers one valuable slice. The job is not to widen it
   to cover all energy work — it is to **package the slice you have into 2–3 repeatable, sellable
   services and automate delivery.**

---

## 1. Two hard calibrations (from the data)

**A. EnergyLens ≠ "most energy listings."** `[Muhtemel]` A large share of energy freelance work is
physical/engineering and **out of scope**: PV/solar system design (PVsyst, AutoCAD), electrical
engineering, load-flow (ETAP), energy trading/market modelling, IoT/firmware, on-site commissioning.
Bidding these expecting EnergyLens to deliver burns your reputation. Your lane is
**data / analysis / reporting**, not engineering / physical work.

**B. The subsidised German market is GATED.** `[Kesin]` The big subsidised residential chunk — the
iSFP renovation roadmap (€1,500–2,500 per single-family house, BAFA 50%), official Energieausweis,
BAFA-funded Energieberatung — legally requires a **dena-listed Energie-Effizienz-Experte** (≈22,800
registered). If you are not listed, you **cannot deliver the funded deliverable solo.** This is
decisive for the offer (§4).

## 3. Recommended productized menu — the 2–3, not "all"

Selected on: *sells × EnergyLens-does × you-can-deliver-legally-solo × compounds-to-wedge.*

1. **Energy data analytics & BI dashboards** — the **cash engine.** Uses your exact Power BI / DAX /
   Fabric expertise; fastest to sell; immediate. *(Caveat from the data: clients distrust freelancers
   on governance-heavy dashboards — counter by selling EnergyLens as a productized, repeatable asset,
   not ad-hoc one-off files.)*
2. **ESG / EPBD decision-support reporting** (unregulated) — the **identity / wedge engine.** Portfolio
   carbon + EPBD-readiness + CapEx prioritisation + subsidy ROI, delivered as an "ESRS-aligned"
   decision-support report. Recurring (annual/quarterly). The SME-priced-out-of-Predium gap.
3. **(Companion) ongoing monitoring/benchmarking subscription** — the **proto-SaaS bridge:** converts
   gig #1/#2 clients into recurring revenue.

All three are **one pipeline:** ingest building/energy data → analyse/benchmark → dashboard + report.
That pipeline *is* EnergyLens. Nothing new to build — only to **package + automate.**

---

## 4. The qualification gate → the white-label redirect

`[Muhtemel]` The certified market (iSFP / Energieausweis) is the largest subsidised demand but closed
to you solo. So don't compete with the 22,800 Energieberater — **sell to them.** A white-label
EnergyLens that speeds their DIN EN 16247 / iSFP analysis turns the gate into a B2B channel (your
3-pillar Pillar-1 idea). Recurring, qualification-free for you, and it rides their existing *funded*
demand instead of fighting for it.

---

## 5. What each service needs from the system (gig-driven, minimal)

- **Report-factory automation** (raw client data → QA'd, branded client PDF) — the one automation that
  matters; build only what the first paid gig actually reveals.
- **Plausibility / QA flags** (defensibility — "output = draft" guardrail).
- **Client-deliverable templates** (vs internal dashboards).
- Nothing else until a paying gig demands it.

---

## 6. Open confirmations (product owner)

1. **Your dena / EEE status:** are you a registered Energie-Effizienz-Experte (or planning to get
   listed)? If **yes**, the certified iSFP market opens directly. If **no**, §4 white-label is the
   path. *(Determines a whole revenue line — answer first.)*
2. **Lead offer:** cash-lead with BI/analytics + identity-lead with ESG/EPBD reporting — confirm or
   re-weight.
