## 1. Asansör konuşması (İngilizce)

**10 saniye** ("ne yapıyorsun?"):
> "I'm Ali Mert. I built EnergyLens, a software platform that helps building portfolios cut energy cost and carbon and stay ahead of EU rules like EPBD, without installing any hardware. It works from the bills and certificates a building already has."

**30 saniye** (ilgi varsa):
> "I'm an energy engineer, I just finished my M.Sc. here in Berlin, and I built EnergyLens on my own. It's an estimation-first energy and compliance layer for commercial and residential portfolios. The point is you don't need sensors or smart meters to start. It runs on existing data like bills, meters and EPCs, and gives you energy intensity, carbon, CRREM stranding, EPBD risk, and ranked retrofit measures with subsidy-adjusted ROI. Every number is shown as a range with its assumptions. It's built and live, and right now I'm talking to people in the field to see where it's most useful and where I can contribute."

> Son cümle ("where it's most useful and where I can contribute") çift kapı açar: hem partner/pilot hem iş. Hiçbir kapıyı kapatmaz.

**Dürüstlük çıpası:** "built and live, lining up first pilots." Müşteri/gelir UYDURMA. Sorulursa: "solo for now, incorporating as I line up the first pilots."
